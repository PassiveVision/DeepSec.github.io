About Me

I am a PhD student working on AI-related cybersecurity. Especially, I am currently working on adversarial example attack on deep learning based systems. My other research interests include deep neural network, applied machine learning, decision making system, and complex network.

I am currently with Swinburne University of Technology and CSIRO Data61, Australia.
