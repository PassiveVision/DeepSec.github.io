About Me

I received my Bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Technology, China. I worked towards my doctoral degree in Swinburne University and CSIRO Data61. I am currently a CERC postdoctoral fellow with CSIRO Data61, Australia. I am working under Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence Future Science Platform, CSIRO.

My primary research interest resides in adversarial machine learning and AI-related cybersecurity. My other research interests include deep neural networks, applied machine learning, decision-making systems, and complex networks. I play drums in my spare time since 2013. Table tennis is my favourite weekly sport.
